COVID Guidelines

Cadets & Students


We are incorporating all USG/GT coronavirus guidelines into our classes. See below for a list of safety precautions:

  • Temperature checks and COVID Questionnaire upon entry to the 2nd floor of the O’Keefe building
  • Masks are required in classes where 6 ft of separation cannot be maintained, please bring a mask to every class (lecture, lab, and PT)
  • Room capacity has been reduced
  • The Cadet Wing has been split into two groups to mitigate large crowds
  • Routine cleaning of classrooms and space by GT facilities

Visitors


We are incorporating all USG/GT coronavirus guidelines. If you are visiting the Detachment for testing, recruiting, etc. you must abide by the below safety precautions:

  • Visitors by appointment only
    • Please use the contact information below to set-up an appointment even if it is last minute.
  • Temperature checks and COVID Questionnaire upon entry to the 2nd floor of the O’Keefe building
  • Masks are required by ALL visitors and all others who accompany them (parents, recruiters, etc. included)
