Our Staff

Meet the people who keep this Detachment running

Cadre are active-duty Air Force personnel who come from a variety of background and careers in the Air Force to help shape the service's future leaders.

The officers are Georgia Tech faculty and teach the AFROTC classes, oversee the cadet leadership's planning and execution of activities like PT and LLAB, and provide mentorship and guidance.

The non-commissioned officers serve in essential administrative capacities and ensure cadets complete all of the program requirements to graduate from AFROTC.
